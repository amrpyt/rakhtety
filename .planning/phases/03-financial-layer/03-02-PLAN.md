## Formula rules

- total_cost = fees + profit from `workflow_steps`
- total_paid = payments - refunds plus/minus adjustments
- outstanding_debt = total_cost - total_paid
- planned_profit = sum workflow step profit
- realized_profit = payment profit portion already collected
</context>
